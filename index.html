Iddings Trucking, Inc. is a regional and over the  road transporter of dry bulk commodities such as metals, ores, coal, sand, gravel, lime, cement, fertilizers, plastics, and silica fume dust in dump and pneumatic tank trailers.  


Situated in the Marietta, Ohio / Parkersburg, West Virginia area of the Mid-Ohio Valley, the company serves customers of all sizes throughout Ohio and West Virginia. We deliver regionally and long-haul across the U.S. and Canada. 


Iddings Trucking, Inc. prides itself on the service it has provided for over 45 years, with courteous, on-time pickup and delivery at competitive rates.  The company has always put its customers first, and as a result, is still doing business with its very first customer.
