Iddings Trucking, Inc. was formed in May of 1966 by Howard W. Iddings and George C. Loeber.  The company grew and soon Raymond E. Waters joined the firm.  Thomas E. Hall joined the firm in the 1970's when his company merged with Iddings Trucking, Inc.  Mr. Loeber also left the company in the early 1970's, striking out on his own when he started Dry Bulk Transport, Inc.


The company continued to grow, and in the early 1980's, Mr. Loeber rejoined the firm with the merger of Iddings Trucking, Inc. and Dry Bulk Transport, Inc.  It was during this time that the company began to move into the pneumatic tank business.  The addition of pneumatic tank capabilities fueled the growth of the company through the 1980's, during which time it more than doubled in size. 


Mr. Iddings retired from the business at the end of 1991.  Through the 1990's, the company continued to grow.  Today, Iddings Trucking, Inc. is handling the challenges of the new millennium, looking forward to the future while maintaining the customer service and professionalism which built the company in the 20th century.
